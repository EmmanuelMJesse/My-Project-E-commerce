Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most common types of spare parts in the Kenyan market? +

The most common types of spare parts include filters, brake pads, spark plugs, belts, and batteries.

Where can I buy genuine spare parts in Kenya? +

Genuine spare parts can be purchased from authorized dealers, specialized auto parts shops, or through our store.

How can I tell if a spare part is genuine? +

Check for the manufacturer's branding, packaging quality, and hologram or serial number.

Do you offer warranties on spare parts? +

Yes, we offer warranties on most of our products. The warranty period varies depending on the part.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept M-Pesa, credit cards, bank transfers, and cash on delivery.

Can I return or exchange a spare part? +

Yes, returns and exchanges are accepted within 14 days of purchase with the original receipt.

How long does delivery take? +

Delivery within Nairobi takes 1-2 days, while upcountry deliveries take 3-5 days.

Do you provide installation services? +

Yes, we offer installation services for most of our spare parts at an additional cost.

What are your operating hours? +

We operate Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.
